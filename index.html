Red Hot Chili Peppers Fan Page

The Red Hot Chili Peppers, a quintessential SoCal band originally formed in 1983 in Los Angeles, known for their combination of funk and punk rock. The band has rotated in members over the years, the founding members consist of Anthony Kiedis, Michael Balzary, and Hillel Slovak with only Balzary and Kiedis remaining in the band today due to the early death of Slovak. Originally forged from high school friendships, The Red Hot Chili Peppers play whimsical childlike melodies that can be filled with complex sorrow, highlighting the bands tragic past.
